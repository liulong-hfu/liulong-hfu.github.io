About

Long Liu, Ph.D., Assistant Professor at Hefei University, has conducted research at Central South University, Hanyang University in South Korea, and the University of Houston in the United States. He earned his Ph.D. from Central South University under the supervision of Professor Bing Yi. As a jointly trained Ph.D. student, he studied at Hanyang University under Professor Gil-Ho Yoon (ISSMO member) and at the University of Houston under Professor Zhigang Deng. Dr. Long Liu specializes in structural topology optimization design, with a focus on multi-field coupled meta-structure optimization design methods and structural measurement. He has published over 30 academic papers in prestigious international journals such as Additive Manufacturing, Composite Structures, Computers & Structures, Computer-Aided Design, and Measurement.

I am constantly looking for self-motivated undergraduate and master students to join my group. If you are interested in academic research or competition, please get in touch with me directly.
